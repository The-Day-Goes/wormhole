How To:

  • Computer:
    • Use the WASD keys to move.
    • Use the mouse to look around.
    • Press F11 to enter fullscreen.
  • Mobile:
    • Swipe to move/look around.
    • Tap "lift restricted movement" to
      look around by moving your device.
